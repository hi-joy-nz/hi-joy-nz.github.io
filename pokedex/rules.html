The Pokédex: Server Rules

1. Follow Discord's TOS

These can be found here: discord.com/terms

2. Be Respectful

Treat other members with respect.

Do not insult, offend, put down, or be rude towards other members.

3. Limited Swearing

Keep swearing to an absolute minimum.

Excessive swearing may lead to mutes, warns, or kicks.

Slurs of any kind are unacceptable and will result in a week-long mute, followed by a ban.

4. No Spamming

Spamming of any form is not allowed. This includes but is not limited to:

sending several messages quickly,
sending walls of text,
pinging roles or members repeatedly.

5. No NSFW Content

No NSFW (Not Safe For Work) content is allowed whatsoever.

Discussions of such topics should be kept in other servers or in private messages.

NSFW content will result in heavy punishment.

6. English Only

Please keep conversations in English only, this helps us to moderate the server.

If you wish to speak in other languages, an English translation is required within the message.

7. No Discrimination

Discrimination on the grounds of race, sexuality, gender, or anything else is not allowed under any conditions.

8. No Politics

Politics should be reserved for other servers or private messages, unless a staff member has given explicit permission.

9. Appropriate Nicknames

Nicknames must be appropriate, meaning nothing NSFW or offensive.

Nicknames should also not use fancy font or messy characters.

10. Don't Annoy People Intentionally

Do not waste people's time or annoy them intentionally.

11. No Scams

Attempting to scam members of money, credentials, or personal information is strictly prohibited.

Members found to be scamming other members will be banned immediately.

12. Listen to Staff

Staff have the final say, please do as they ask.
