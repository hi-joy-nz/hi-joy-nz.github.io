DexBot Privacy Policy

Last updated 14th of January, 2025

Data Collection

The only user-related data collected by DexBot are as follows:

* Your Discord user ID

* What Pokémon you have marked as your favourite via /favourite

* Your active shiny hunt and the number of encounters you're at, as set by /shinyhunt

Data Usage

The data, as listed above, is used solely for the core operations of DexBot

No third parties have access to any data collected by DexBot

Removal of Data

You may at any point request the removal of your data (as listed above) via the Pokédex Discord server through a ticket

Contact

If you have any questions regarding our Terms, please contact us though the Pokédex Discord server through a ticket
